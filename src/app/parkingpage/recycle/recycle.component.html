Recycling :

Our Parking areas are equipped with a sustainable approach where we utilize recycled plastics for various purposes. As part of our environmental commitment, we incorporate the use o recycled plastics in our package lot infrastructure.

The roads/flooring in our parking are recycled plastics and polyesters. The recycling process:

  • We shred them into small pieces
  • Adding heated blue metal
  • Melting with the brick jelly
  • Adding Bitumen
  • Laying the Road

By utilizing recycled plastics in our parking lot, we aim to reduce waste and promote a circular economy.
